2. Financial Split, Service Fees, & Stripe Connect

  • The 70/30 Revenue Share: For every completed ride transaction executed via the platform, you agree to pay Totali Inc. a 30% Service Fee (inclusive of HST) for facilitating the transactions as your Authorized Booking and Payments Agent. You invoice the Rider 100 percent of the Rider's Total fare (inclusive of HST).
  • Automated Settlement via Stripe Express: To facilitate this agent relationship, all electronic transactions are split natively at the moment of payment via Stripe Connect Express. Stripe automatically routes your 70% share of the gross fare directly to your Connected Account, while deducting and transferring our 30% Service Fee directly to the Totali Inc. platform account.
  • 100% Cashless Mandate: You are strictly prohibited from accepting cash payments from riders. All billing must flow through your Authorized Agent (Totali Inc.) via the secure application gateway. Cash collection will result in immediate and permanent termination of platform access.
