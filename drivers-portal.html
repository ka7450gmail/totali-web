The 70/30 Revolution.

To the Drivers of London and the Ontario Rideshare Community,

When we sat down to build a No Tips Rideshare™, we didn't start with an app—we started with a calculator.

By capping our platform fee at 30%, we ensure that you earn a fair share. No math, no pressure, just the ride.

Driver Bill of Rights

Our Commitment To You

We believe in transparency and fairness. Here are the core pillars of our driver agreement:

  • Fair Pay: You keep 70% of every fare, every time.
  • No Tip Pressure: We've removed the awkwardness for you and the rider.
  • Local Focus: We are built by Londoners, for Londoners.
  • Safety First: Modern tech to keep you protected on the road.
  • Support: Real people to help you, not just an automated bot.
