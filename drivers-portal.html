From The Founder

The 70/30 Revolution.

To the Drivers of London and the Ontario Rideshare Community,

When we sat down to build a No Tips Rideshare™, we didn't start with an app—we started with a calculator.

For years, the rideshare industry has operated on a model that feels increasingly broken. Drivers are squeezed by rising costs and shrinking percentages, often relying on the uncertainty of tips just to break even. Meanwhile, riders face "tip fatigue" and social pressure every time they reach their destination.

"We knew there had to be a better way: Better Math."

We founded this company on a single, unwavering principle: The 70/30 Advantage™. By capping our platform fee at 30%, we ensure that the you, the person behind the wheel—the Independent Contractor, the local professional—incurring the majority of the expenses, can earn a fair, predictable, and dignified share of every fare.

Because you, our drivers are paid fairly from the start, we’ve been able to do something radical for our riders: We’ve removed tipping entirely from the Apps. When they ride with us, the price they see is the total price they pay. No math, no pressure, just the ride. We call it Zero-Friction Travel.

We are launching here in London on July 1st because this is our home. As we expand across Waterloo Region, Niagara Region, Hamilton, and Windsor through 2027, our mission remains the same: to prove that a rideshare company can be both a sophisticated platform and a fair local partner.
