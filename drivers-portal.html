From The Founder

The 70/30 Revolution.

To the Drivers of London and the Ontario Rideshare Community,

When we sat down to build a No Tips Rideshare™, we didn't start with an app—we started with a calculator.

For years, the industry has relied on the uncertainty of tips. Drivers are squeezed by rising costs and shrinking percentages. We knew there had to be a better way: Better Math.

By capping our platform fee at 30%, we ensure that you, the local professional, earn a fair and dignified share of every fare. We call it Zero-Friction Travel.

We are launching here in London on July 1st because this is our home. Our mission remains to prove that a rideshare company can be both a sophisticated platform and a fair local partner.
