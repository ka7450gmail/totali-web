From The Founder

The 70/30 Revolution.

To the Drivers of London and the Ontario Rideshare Community,

When we sat down to build a No Tips Rideshare™, we didn't start with an app—we started with a calculator.

For years, the industry has relied on the uncertainty of tips. Drivers are squeezed by rising costs and shrinking percentages. Meanwhile, riders face "tip fatigue."

"We knew there had to be a better way: Better Math."

We founded this company on a single, unwavering principle: The 70/30 Advantage™. By capping our platform fee at 30%, we ensure that you, the Independent Contractor, can earn a fair share of every fare.

Because you are paid fairly, we have removed tipping entirely. No math, no pressure, just the ride. We call it Zero-Friction Travel.

The Founder
CEO & Co-founder, Totali
