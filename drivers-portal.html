OFFICIAL MEMO: LONDON LAUNCH

To the Drivers of London and the Ontario Rideshare Community,

When we sat down to build a No Tips Rideshare™, we didn't start with an app—we started with a calculator.

For years, the rideshare industry has operated on a model that feels increasingly broken. Drivers are squeezed by rising costs and shrinking percentages, often relying on the uncertainty of tips just to break even.

We founded this company on a single, unwavering principle: The 70/30 Advantage™. By capping our platform fee at 30%, we ensure that you, the person behind the wheel, can earn a fair and dignified share of every fare.

Because you are paid fairly from the start, we have removed tipping entirely. When they ride with us, the price they see is the total price they pay. No math, no pressure, just the ride.
