Official Memo: London Launch

The 70/30 Revolution.

To the Drivers of London and the Ontario Rideshare Community,

When we sat down to build a No Tips Rideshare™, we didn't start with an app—we started with a calculator.

For years, the rideshare industry has operated on a model that feels increasingly broken. Drivers are squeezed by rising costs and shrinking percentages.

"We knew there had to be a better way: Better Math."

We founded this company on a single, unwavering principle: The 70/30 Advantage™. By capping our platform fee at 30%, we ensure that you earn a fair share of every fare.

Because you are paid fairly, we’ve removed tipping entirely. We call it Zero-Friction Travel.

The Totali Team
