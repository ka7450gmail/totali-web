From The Founder

The 70/30 Revolution.

To the Drivers of London and the Ontario Rideshare Community,

When we sat down to build a No Tips Rideshare™, we didn't start with an app—we started with a calculator.

For years, the industry has relied on the uncertainty of tips. Drivers are squeezed by rising costs and shrinking percentages. Meanwhile, riders face "tip fatigue" and social pressure.

"We knew there had to be a better way: Better Math."

We founded this company on a single, unwavering principle: The 70/30 Advantage™. By capping our platform fee at 30%, we ensure that you, the Independent Contractor, earn a fair and dignified share of every fare.

Because you are paid fairly, we’ve removed tipping entirely from the Apps. When they ride with us, the price they see is the total price they pay. We call it Zero-Friction Travel.

We are launching here in London on July 1st because this is our home. Our mission is to prove that a rideshare company can be both a sophisticated platform and a fair local partner.

The Founder
CEO & Co-founder, Totali
