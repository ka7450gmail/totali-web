TOTALI RIDES – PRIVACY POLICY

Last Updated: June 17, 2026

Totali Inc., operating as Totali Rides ("Totali Rides", "we", "us"), is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy is designed to comply with Canada’s Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA) and applicable provincial privacy legislation.

1. Accountability & Information We Collect

We collect and manage personal information strictly required to operate our rideshare technology platform safely and efficiently. This includes:

  • Rider Data: Name, email address, phone number, and tokenized billing details managed securely by our payment processor (Stripe).
  • Driver Data: Name, contact details, social insurance number (for Stripe tax reporting), banking payout tokens, driver’s license, vehicle registration, background check results, and insurance abstractions.
  • Real-Time Geo-Location Data: We track precise background and foreground GPS location data from both rider and driver mobile devices to facilitate safe dispatching, tracking, routing, and accurate fare calculations.

2. Consent and Purpose of Collection

By registering an account and using our mobile applications, you explicitly consent to the collection, tracking, use, and disclosure of your personal data as outlined in this policy. We use your data exclusively to:

  • Match riders with nearby independent drivers.
  • Process automated cashless transactions and revenue distributions.
  • Verify driver municipal compliance, safety credentials, and insurance status.
  • Investigate platform safety disputes, insurance claims, or traffic incidents.

3. Limiting Use, Disclosure, and Retention

Totali Rides does not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third-party marketing companies. Your data is only shared with authorized vendors strictly necessary for operations (e.g., Stripe for payment split routing, and municipal authorities when legally mandated under local bylaws). We retain your data only for as long as your account remains active or as required by Canadian federal tax laws and corporate accounting regulations.

4. Safeguards & Data Storage

We deploy robust, industry-standard administrative, physical, and electronic security safeguards to prevent data breaches, unauthorized access, or loss of information. While data may be processed through secure cloud infrastructure, all transactions and operational logs are monitored under strict privacy protocols. Please note that no transmission over the internet can be guaranteed 100% secure.

5. Individual Access & Openness

Under PIPEDA, you possess the absolute right to request access to the personal information Totali Rides holds on your file. You may update or correct your profile data directly inside the application interface at any time. If you wish to delete your account or withdraw your consent for location tracking, you may submit a request to our privacy desk, though removing location access will terminate your ability to use the active dispatch platform.

6. Contacting Our Privacy Officer

If you have questions regarding our compliance with PIPEDA, or wish to file a request regarding your stored personal data files, please direct your inquiry directly to our corporate compliance team via the administrative contact channels listed inside the application platform.
